Rooted in Nature

Sourcing from Lonavla's Finest Farm

At Made of Milk, we believe in the power of nature, which helps us stay connected to our roots. We source our ingredients from one Farm, nestled in the misty greens of Lonavla, where healthy and happy breeds of cows and buffaloes are treated with utmost care.

Pure and Natural

Our Commitment to Quality Milk

We are committed to delivering products that are free from harmful pesticides, antibiotics and synthetic flavors. Each sip, each bite, we guarantee the highest quality of natural goodness of organically sourced milk.

Delivery Process

step 01

Order Placement

Customers place their dairy orders through our user-friendly online platform or by phone, specifying their desired products and quantities.

step 02

Processing & Packaging

Upon receiving orders, our team promptly processes them, carefully selecting and packaging the requested dairy items with utmost attention to quality and freshness.

step 03

Delivery Coordination

Once packaged, orders are handed over to our logistics partners for efficient delivery scheduling. Our dedicated team ensures seamless coordination to guarantee timely and hassle-free delivery to customers' doorsteps.

With our commitment to customer satisfaction, orders are swiftly dispatched for delivery, ensuring that customers receive their dairy products promptly and in optimal condition, ready to enjoy.
